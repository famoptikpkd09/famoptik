I’m not sure what type of spectacles I need. How do I know which lens is right for me?

The optician will guide you through a comprehensive eye test and evaluate your lifestyle needs. They can recommend single-vision, progressive, or digital lenses depending on whether you need them for reading, distance, or both. Premium shops often provide high-quality lenses with coatings that suit your everyday comfort.

Are there spectacles that feel comfortable all day without being heavy?

Yes! Modern optical shops offer lightweight frames made of materials like titanium, memory metal, or acetate. When paired with high-index lenses, even strong prescriptions feel comfortable, and the fit can be customized for your face.

Can I get spectacles that protect my eyes from sunlight and screens?

Absolutely. Premium lenses often come with UV protection for outdoor use and blue-light filtering coatings for digital screens. Some lenses are even photochromic, adjusting automatically to light changes for extra comfort.

How do I maintain my spectacles so they last longer?

A luxury optical shop will advise you to clean lenses with a microfiber cloth and lens solution, store them in a protective case, and avoid harsh chemicals. Regular check-ups ensure your prescription remains accurate and the lenses stay scratch-free.

What makes premium spectacles different from regular ones?

Premium spectacles combine precision optics, advanced coatings, and designer frames. They reduce glare, minimize eye strain, are more durable, and offer elegant, customized fitting—making them both functional and stylish.
